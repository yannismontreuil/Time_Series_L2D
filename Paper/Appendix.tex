\label{app:roadmap}

\begin{itemize}
    \item Section~\ref{algo}: end-to-end router/filtering pseudocode and optional learning updates.
    \item Section~\ref{app:info_gain}: information-gain derivations used for IDS.
    \item Appendix~\ref{app:planning_relative_threshold}: predictive scheduling details.
    \item Appendix~\ref{app:cross_covariance}: exact (non-factorized) Kalman update cross-covariance.
    \item Appendix~\ref{app:proof_cross_update}--\ref{app:transfer}: proofs of propositions.
\end{itemize}
